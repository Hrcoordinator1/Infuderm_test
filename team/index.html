Meet our Team

Expert care from a team that values safety, comfort, and natural results.

Nurse Practitioner

Angelica Briceño

Master Injector | Aesthetic Medicine & Wellness

Angelica Briceño is a Doctor of Nursing Practice (DNP) and Board-Certified Nurse Practitioner, with foundational training as a Medical Doctor from the Central University of Venezuela. She holds advanced postgraduate education in Aesthetic Medicine from Tech University Medical School in Spain and Healthcare Informatics, reflecting a strong commitment to innovation, clinical precision, and evidence-based care.

With many years of experience in cosmetic dermatology, advanced medical lasers, and aesthetic injectables, Dr. Briceño delivers elevated, natural-looking results through a refined blend of science, technology, and artistic precision. Her expertise also includes advanced medical weight management using GLP-1–based therapies, supporting patients in achieving sustainable transformations as part of a comprehensive approach to aesthetics and wellness.

Nurse Practitioner

Kopila Sangroula Pandey

MSN, AG-ACNP-BC · Expert Injector | Facial Balancing & Natural Rejuvenation

Kopila Sangroula Pandey is an Acute Care Nurse Practitioner with experience and expertise in aesthetics and wellness. She specializes in neurotoxin treatments, facial balancing, natural-looking aesthetic enhancements, and personalized treatment plans designed to refresh and rejuvenate while preserving each patient's unique beauty.

With a background in acute care, allergy, immunology, and aesthetic medicine, Kopila combines clinical expertise with an artistic eye to create balanced, harmonious results. Her philosophy focuses on enhancing natural features, promoting healthy skin, and helping patients look refreshed, confident, and authentically themselves.
