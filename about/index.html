Our Story

A clinic built on
precision & safety.

INFUDERM is designed for patients who want a polished aesthetic experience with medical precision and absolute safety. Our goal is to provide personalized, results-driven aesthetic treatments that help every client feel confident, refreshed, and empowered.

We hold every treatment to the highest clinical standards — beginning with a careful consultation and thorough safety screening before any procedure begins. This commitment to evidence-based care ensures that each patient receives a treatment plan truly aligned with their goals, anatomy, and long-term skin health.

Our team brings together Board-Certified Nurse Practitioners, Registered Nurses, and Licensed Estheticians with deep expertise in advanced aesthetics, wellness, and skin care. Every member of the INFUDERM team shares the same philosophy: natural-looking results, delivered safely.

From IV therapy and GLP-1 weight management to Morpheus8, HydraFacial, and laser hair removal, our services are curated around what delivers meaningful, lasting improvement — not trends. We serve patients at our two locations in Reston and Aldie, Virginia, and are proud to be a trusted name in Northern Virginia medical aesthetics.

Mission

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a harmonious balance between aesthetics and wellness by combining advanced medical technology with evidence-based treatments and expert clinical care. We strive to enhance natural beauty, promote skin health, and deliver refined, lasting results in a safe, ethical, and patient-centered environment.

Our Vision

Our vision is to advance the future of aesthetic medicine by continuously integrating the latest technologies and evidence-based innovations. We strive to be recognized as a center of excellence where clinical precision, safety, and artistry converge to deliver superior, natural-looking outcomes.
