Overview

What is Triton Laser Hair Removal?

Triton is an advanced laser hair removal technology by InMode designed to target hair follicles at different depths within the skin. Unlike traditional single-wavelength laser systems, Triton uses multi-wavelength technology, allowing providers to treat different depths of hair follicles more effectively.

This makes it suitable for a wide range of skin tones, hair types, and treatment needs across facial and body areas. The laser energy is absorbed by the pigment in the hair, gently heating the follicle and reducing its ability to produce new hair over time.

Triton is also designed for efficiency — most sessions, even those targeting large areas, take no longer than 30 minutes. Because hair grows in cycles, a series of treatment sessions is typically recommended to achieve optimal long-term hair reduction results.

Tired of Constant Hair Removal?

Most people don't start laser hair removal for luxury — they start because shaving, waxing, and threading become inconvenient over time. Common concerns include frequent shaving routines, skin irritation after waxing, ingrown hairs, uneven hair regrowth, and time-consuming maintenance.

The Science Behind Triton

How Does Triton Laser Work?

Triton laser hair removal works on the principle of selective photothermolysis — the laser energy targets the pigment (melanin) in the hair follicle, converts light into heat, and gradually reduces the follicle's ability to grow hair over time.

Contraindications & Precautions

While Triton laser hair removal is safe for most individuals, there are certain situations where treatment may need to be postponed. Treatment may not be appropriate if you have:

  • Active skin infections or open wounds in the treatment area
  • Significant skin irritation or inflammation
  • Recent excessive sun exposure or sunburn
  • Pregnancy or nursing (treatment is typically postponed)
  • Photosensitivity or light-sensitive conditions
  • Medications that increase skin sensitivity

A consultation at INFUDERM ensures your treatment is safe, appropriate, and fully personalized.

Downtime & Recovery: One of the advantages of Triton laser hair removal is that it requires little to no downtime. Most patients are able to return to their normal daily routine immediately after treatment. Some mild and temporary effects may include slight redness, mild warmth in the treated area, or temporary sensitivity — these typically resolve within a few hours. Most patients find Triton laser hair removal comfortable and well-tolerated, with advanced cooling technology designed to enhance comfort throughout the session.
