Replenishment Delivered Directly

What is IV Therapy?

IV Therapy delivers fluids, vitamins, minerals, and nutrients directly into the bloodstream through an intravenous infusion. Because nutrients bypass the digestive system, they are delivered efficiently and directly to the body.

Many patients choose IV Therapy to support hydration, recovery, wellness, immune health, energy levels, and nutrient replenishment.

Targeted Nutrient Support

What are Wellness Injections?

Wellness Injections, also known as IM (intramuscular) injections, deliver nutrients directly into muscle tissue for efficient absorption. They are often chosen by individuals looking for convenient wellness support without the longer appointment time associated with IV infusions.

What to Expect

What Happens During Your Session?

01

Consultation

We'll review your health history, wellness goals, current medications, and individual needs to ensure treatment is safe and appropriate for you.

02

Preparation

You'll relax comfortably while your selected IV formulation is prepared by our clinical team.

03

Treatment

A small IV catheter is gently placed into a vein, typically in the arm, and your infusion begins. Most sessions take approximately 30–60 minutes.

04

Monitoring

Our team will monitor you throughout your session to ensure a comfortable and safe experience.

05

Completion

Once your infusion is complete, the IV is removed and you may return to most normal activities immediately.

Before Your Visit

How to Prepare

Arrive well-hydrated whenever possible
Eat a light meal before your visit
Bring a list of current medications and supplements
Wear comfortable clothing with easy arm access

After Treatment

Post-Treatment Care

Continue drinking water throughout the day
Follow any recommendations from your provider
Maintain healthy nutrition and lifestyle habits
Contact our team if you have any post-treatment questions

Safety & Medical Screening: IV Therapy is generally well tolerated when administered by qualified healthcare professionals following an appropriate consultation and evaluation. However, treatment may not be appropriate for everyone. Additional evaluation may be required for individuals with certain heart conditions, kidney disorders, conditions affecting fluid balance, certain medication considerations, or active infections. During your consultation, we'll review your medical history to help determine whether IV Therapy or Wellness Injections are appropriate for you.
