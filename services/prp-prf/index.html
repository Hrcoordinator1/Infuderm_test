Overview

What is PRP & PRF Therapy?

PRP and PRF are regenerative treatments that harness your body's own growth factors to support natural healing, collagen production, and tissue renewal. From hair restoration and facial rejuvenation to under-eye treatments and combination aesthetic procedures, both options offer a natural approach to aesthetic improvement.

PRP

Platelet-Rich Plasma

Natural Rejuvenation

PRP is a regenerative treatment that uses a concentrated portion of your own blood containing platelets and growth factors. After a small blood sample is collected, it is processed to isolate the platelet-rich plasma before being applied or injected into the treatment area.

  • Concentrated platelets & growth factors
  • Widely used in aesthetic medicine
  • Processed with anticoagulants
  • Commonly used for skin and hair concerns
PRF

Platelet-Rich Fibrin

Next-Generation Regenerative Treatment

PRF is an advanced regenerative treatment derived from your own blood. Similar to PRP, PRF contains platelets and growth factors — but processed differently to create a fibrin matrix that allows for a slower release of growth factors over time.

  • Platelets, growth factors & fibrin matrix
  • Newer regenerative treatment option
  • Processed without anticoagulants
  • Slower, sustained growth factor release

PRP vs. PRF — What's the Difference?
