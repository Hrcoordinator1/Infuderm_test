Overview

What is HydraFacial®?

HydraFacial® is a non-invasive skin rejuvenation treatment that deeply cleanses, exfoliates, extracts impurities, and hydrates the skin using patented vortex technology. It is one of the most versatile skin treatments available — suitable for a wide range of skin types and concerns.

Whether you're looking to refresh dull skin, improve hydration, or maintain a healthy glow, HydraFacial offers a personalized treatment experience suitable for a variety of skin concerns — with little to no downtime.

At INFUDERM, every HydraFacial begins with a personalized consultation to understand your skin type, lifestyle factors, current skincare routine, individual concerns, and long-term goals.

The Process

How Does HydraFacial Work?

HydraFacial follows a proven multi-step process that deeply cleanses the skin, removes congestion, and infuses nourishing serums to improve hydration and overall skin health.

01

Cleanse + Exfoliate

Dead skin cells and impurities are gently removed from the skin's surface, revealing a fresher, smoother complexion underneath.

02

Gentle Peel

A mild resurfacing solution helps loosen debris and congestion from pores without causing discomfort or irritation.

03

Extraction

Vortex suction technology helps clear congested pores painlessly — removing blackheads, oil, and buildup more effectively than manual extraction.

04

Hydration

The skin is infused with hydrating and nourishing serums tailored to your skin type and concerns, leaving skin plump and refreshed.

05

Protection

Treatment concludes with customized skincare recommendations and sun protection guidance to help maintain your results.

Post-Treatment Guidance

HydraFacial Aftercare Tips

First 24 Hours

  • Avoid excessive heat (saunas, steam rooms, hot showers)
  • Avoid harsh exfoliants or active skincare products
  • Stay well hydrated throughout the day
  • Follow all provider recommendations closely

Following Days

  • Wear SPF 30+ daily to protect your skin
  • Continue using hydration-focused skincare products
  • Protect your skin from unnecessary irritation
  • Contact our team if you have any questions or concerns

Side Effects & Safety: Most patients experience little to no downtime after HydraFacial. Mild redness or sensitivity may occur immediately after treatment but typically resolves within a few hours. HydraFacial is a gentle, non-invasive treatment that most patients find comfortable and relaxing.
