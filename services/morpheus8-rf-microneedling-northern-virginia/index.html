What is Morpheus8® RF Microneedling?

Morpheus8® is an advanced RF Microneedling treatment that combines traditional Microneedling with radiofrequency (RF) energy to stimulate collagen production and remodel deeper layers of the skin. By delivering RF energy beneath the skin's surface, Morpheus8® helps improve skin texture, fine lines, wrinkles, acne scars, enlarged pores, and mild to moderate skin laxity.

At INFUDERM, Morpheus8® treatments are customized for both men and women seeking non-surgical skin rejuvenation in Reston, Aldie, and Northern Virginia. Every treatment begins with a personalized consultation to evaluate your skin quality, treatment areas, and long-term goals.

Collagen Remodeling From Within

Morpheus8® uses ultra-fine microneedles to create controlled micro-channels within the skin while simultaneously delivering radiofrequency energy into deeper tissue layers. This dual-action approach helps stimulate natural collagen production, elastin production, skin remodeling, tissue tightening, and skin renewal. Over time, the skin becomes firmer, smoother, and more refined.

Who Should Avoid Morpheus8® RF Microneedling?

Morpheus8® may not be appropriate for everyone. Treatment may need to be postponed or modified for individuals with:

  • Active skin infections or open wounds in the treatment area
  • Certain skin conditions
  • Recent sunburn
  • Pregnancy or nursing
  • Certain medical conditions that may affect treatment suitability

A consultation is recommended to determine candidacy and ensure treatment is safe for your individual needs.

Downtime & Recovery: Most patients experience temporary redness, swelling, and mild sensitivity immediately after Morpheus8® treatment. Recovery varies by individual, but many patients return to normal daily activities shortly after while following recommended aftercare instructions. A topical numbing cream is typically applied before treatment to enhance comfort. Most appointments take approximately 45–90 minutes depending on the treatment area.
