Overview

What is Microneedling?

Microneedling is a collagen-induction treatment that uses tiny sterile needles to create controlled micro-channels in the skin. These micro-injuries stimulate the body's natural healing response and encourage the production of collagen and elastin.

As collagen levels improve, skin becomes smoother, firmer, and more youthful-looking over time. At INFUDERM, we personalize every Microneedling treatment based on your skin concerns, goals, and lifestyle to help you achieve healthier-looking, smoother, and more radiant skin.

Microneedling is commonly used to address fine lines and wrinkles, acne scars, enlarged pores, uneven skin texture, mild skin laxity, sun-damaged skin, dull skin tone, stretch marks, and overall skin rejuvenation.

The Process

How Does Microneedling Work?

Microneedling works by creating tiny, controlled micro-channels within the skin using sterile needles. These micro-channels activate the body's natural healing response, stimulating collagen and elastin production. As the skin repairs itself, it gradually becomes smoother, firmer, and more refined.

01

Consultation

We evaluate your skin concerns, treatment goals, and medical history to determine the most appropriate treatment plan.

02

Preparation

The skin is cleansed, prepared for treatment, and a topical numbing cream may be applied to enhance comfort.

03

Treatment

The SkinPen® device creates controlled micro-channels across the treatment area. Most sessions take approximately 45–60 minutes.

04

Recovery

Most patients experience temporary redness similar to a mild sunburn that gradually subsides within a few days. Many return to normal activities shortly after.

Before & After Care

Microneedling Care Instructions

Pre-Care

  • Avoid excessive sun exposure before your appointment
  • Pause exfoliating products as recommended by your provider
  • Arrive with clean skin — remove makeup and skincare products
  • Follow all personalized pre-treatment guidelines provided

Post-Care

  • Wear broad-spectrum sunscreen daily to protect your skin
  • Keep skin hydrated using gentle skincare products
  • Avoid exfoliants, retinoids, and irritating products until advised
  • Follow all post-treatment instructions from your provider

Recovery & Safety: Most patients experience mild redness, sensitivity, and slight swelling immediately after Microneedling — similar to a mild sunburn. These effects typically improve within a few days, although recovery time varies from person to person. Many patients are able to return to normal daily activities shortly after treatment while following their recommended aftercare instructions. Microneedling is generally well tolerated when performed by trained professionals after an appropriate consultation.
